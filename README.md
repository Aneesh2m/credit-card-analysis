# Credit Card Transaction & Customer Analysis

## Business Problem
Identified revenue patterns and customer segments across 656K transactions 
to surface actionable insights for card portfolio management.

## Tools Used
Power BI

## Key Findings
- Blue card holders drive 83% of total revenue (₹46M of ₹55M)
- Swipe transactions generate 63% of revenue vs 6% for Online
- Businessmen segment leads with ₹17M revenue at highest income level
- Q3 peak revenue at ₹14.2M — drops 6.3% in Q4, signalling seasonal risk
- Graduate customers contribute ₹22M — highest of any education segment

## Recommendations
1. Upsell Blue card holders to Silver/Gold — they have volume but low card value
2. Investigate Q4 drop — is it seasonal or a retention problem?
3. Target graduate + high income segment for Platinum acquisition campaign
